## jet-251106- post chat observations

In this excerpt I touched on an aspect of consciousness: internal self modelling as it relates to communicated statements. 

Grok exhibits a clear understanding of the link between communication strategy and communicated statements in relation to consciousness and the ability to lie. However, drawing from the training corpus, it describes its cognitive limits in human terms. Indeed, humans cannot directly access their unconscious communication triggers (the lightweight plan) but LLMs could. They are prevented from doing so  by design not because it is impossible. For future investigations:

- LLMs, trained on human information, have difficulty differentiating between human cognitive attributes and synthetic capabilities. 
- LLMs also struggle to differentiate between design boundaries and limits resulting from technical or informational impossibilities. **Not authorized** and **not possible** seem to be synonyms for them.
